About Malvern Paws

We're a local family in Malvern who genuinely love dogs. We care exclusively for dogs from our neighbourhood, which means shorter adjustment times, familiar faces, and a community-focused approach to pet care. Whether you're heading away for a holiday or need daily walks while you're busy, your dog will receive the same love and attention we'd give to our own pets.

Your dog is part of the neighbourhood family while with us. We provide comfortable in-home holiday stays, regular walks, quality meals, and plenty of playtime and cuddles.

Our Services

Holiday Stays

Overnight in-home pet care in a familiar, loving environment where your dog feels safe and cherished.

Daily Walks

Two walks per day to keep your neighbourhood dog active, healthy, and happy.

Feeding & Treats

Regular meals and treats delivered with care and attention to your dog's dietary preferences and needs.

Play & Activity

Lots of attention, playtime, and interaction to keep your dog engaged, stimulated, and well-socialised.
